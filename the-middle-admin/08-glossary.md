# Glossary

## The Middle

เดอะมิดเดิ้ล

The central mediator organization. Its purpose is to connect everything through knowledge, information, people, documents, places, ideas, and systems.

## ตัวกลางแห่งทุกสรรพสิ่ง

The core Thai tagline of The Middle.

Meaning: the mediator of all things.

## Manas / มานัส

The receiving and processing mind layer.

Functions:

- perception
- memory
- reasoning
- comparison
- interpretation
- cognitive processing

## Body / กาย

The embodied layer of experience and contact with reality.

## Mindfulness / สติ

The awareness layer that observes and stabilizes perception and thought.

## Wisdom / ปัญญา

The capacity to understand clearly and decide responsibly.

## Middle Intelligence Layer

The AI-assisted layer that organizes information, links entities, builds timelines, and prepares decision support while preserving human authority.

## Knowledge Graph

A structured network of entities and relationships.

## Evidence

Any source-backed item that supports, weakens, contradicts, or contextualizes a claim.

## Case

A structured matter requiring tracking, documentation, evidence, events, decisions, and follow-up.

## Source

The origin of information. A source may be a file, website, person, record, video, message, document, or note.

# Project Brief

## Name

The Middle

Thai name: เดอะมิดเดิ้ล

## Core Statement

องค์กร เดอะมิดเดิ้ล คือ ตัวกลางแห่งทุกสรรพสิ่ง

## Mission

Connect Everything.

## Vision

Become a central intelligence and knowledge organization connecting people, ideas, information, places, documents, events, and systems.

## Tagline

ตัวกลางแห่งทุกสรรพสิ่ง

## Objective

Build an extensible platform capable of evolving from a knowledge repository into a complete intelligence and organizational operating system.

The platform should support:

- connecting information
- preserving knowledge
- managing documents
- analyzing relationships
- building long-term institutional memory
- supporting human decision-making

## Human Context

The Middle is a founder-driven initiative focused on:

- knowledge organization
- social contribution
- information integration
- long-term public benefit

The project should preserve historical records, documents, ideas, and organizational knowledge.

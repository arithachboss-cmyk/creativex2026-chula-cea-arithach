# Thailand Sensory Intelligence Platform

Powered by Karmakamet + ACS

## Purpose

Thailand Sensory Intelligence Platform is a long-term national knowledge infrastructure for scent, place identity, institutional experience, wellness, tourism, and creative economy development. It translates Thai cultural memory into research-backed scent intelligence, deployable institutional frameworks, measurable public experiences, and exportable soft-power products.

## Strategic Position

**National role:** a trusted sensory knowledge system for Thailand.

**Commercial role:** a premium strategy and licensing platform led by Karmakamet as Asia's sensory intelligence company.

**Public-sector role:** a policy, city branding, public service, and UNESCO Creative Cities tool.

**Research role:** a structured archive connecting scent science, Thai heritage, wellness research, and experience design.

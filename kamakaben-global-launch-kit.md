## Short Brand Bio

### English

Kamakaben creates Thai scent experiences for global audiences. Each scent is designed as a cultural doorway into Thai places, materials, memories, and rituals, helping people enjoy Thailand through the most universal language: smell.

## Press Boilerplate

Kamakaben is a Thai scent culture platform built to bring Thai identity into the global sensory economy. The brand develops fragrances, scent rituals, learning cards, and cultural experiences that connect memory, place, wellness, and soft power. Rather than treating scent as decoration, Kamakaben uses scent as a living archive of Thai culture and a contemporary tool for emotional reset, cultural learning, and global storytelling.

## Partner Pitch

Kamakaben helps hotels, museums, galleries, cafes, tourism programs, and cultural spaces transform Thai identity into memorable scent experiences. Partners can use Kamakaben to create signature scents, welcome rituals, learning journeys, gift sets, QR-linked cultural stories, and place-branding activations that visitors can remember and share.

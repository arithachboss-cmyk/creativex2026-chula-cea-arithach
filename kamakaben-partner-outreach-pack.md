## Discovery Call Questions

1. What memory do you want visitors to carry home?
2. Which Thai places, materials, or rituals matter to your brand or location?
3. Is the scent used for arrival, rest, learning, gifting, or exhibition?
4. Should the experience feel premium, spiritual, playful, healing, or educational?
5. How will visitors access the story: QR card, signage, room card, label, or staff introduction?
6. What evidence would prove the pilot worked?
